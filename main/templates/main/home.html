How the Mentorship Works

Direct feedback, real strategies, zero sugarcoating; exact steps we'll take to level up your tech skills together.

Step 1: Assessment & Custom Roadmap

We start with a comprehensive evaluation of your current abilities and goals. I'll identify your strengths, knowledge gaps, and create a personalized learning roadmap with weekly milestones—no generic tutorials, everything tailored to your skill level.

Step 2: Weekly 1-on-1 Sessions & Code Reviews

Every week, we'll have focused 60-minute calls to review your progress, debug code together, and tackle challenging concepts. I'll review every line of code you write, providing detailed feedback on best practices and professional coding standards.

Step 3: Hands-on Projects & Portfolio Building

Build real applications that showcase your skills. From responsive websites to full-stack apps, you'll create portfolio pieces that impress employers while learning through practical, real-world development challenges.

Step 4: Career Preparation & Job Readiness

Beyond coding skills, I'll prepare you for the job market. Interview practice, portfolio optimization, resume refinement, and networking strategies to confidently land your first or next developer role.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about the mentorship program? Find answers below

How are mentorship sessions conducted?

Sessions are held via video call (Zoom or Google Meet) at times that work for your schedule. Each session includes screen sharing for code reviews, pair programming, and direct guidance on your projects.

Can I switch plans if needed?

Yes! You can upgrade your plan at any time. If you need to downgrade, you can do so at the end of your current billing cycle. I'm flexible and want to ensure you're in the plan that best meets your needs.

What technologies do you teach?

I specialize in modern web development technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and more. Whether you're interested in frontend, backend, or full-stack development, I can tailor your learning path accordingly.

Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?

Yes! If you're not satisfied after your first mentorship session, I offer a 100% money-back guarantee. Your success and satisfaction are my top priorities.

How do payments work?

Payments are processed monthly through secure payment gateways including PayPal, Stripe, or bank transfer. You'll receive an invoice before each payment, and automatic billing can be set up for convenience.
